Week 12:

Wildcard

For week 12, the homework was to create a mold and cast.

To create the mold, I used a lego car that I had readily available. I then took a box and sealed the edges with duct tape. Following the instructions on Nathan's website, I first hot glued my lego car to the bottom of the container.


Next, I estimated I needed about 200cc of the liquid to cover my car entirely. This ended up being all of the provided solutions (each being 100cc).

After mixing the solutions, I filled the container with them. I waited about 8 hours, and after everything dried, I ripped the container apart to expose the mold.


It was tough for me to get the lego car out of the mold. My spontaneous nature caused me to overthink the process of removing the car. After breaking the car up into pieces, I finally pulled the car from the mold. However, the mold was not useable because the cast would be in one piece and impossible to remove.
