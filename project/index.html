Project



My final project was to create an RC plane. I started working on the project during week 6, when I created most of the modeling of the aircraft.

Before week 6, I had many different ideas for a final project. However, I realized that something that I always wanted was an RC plane. I never had a chance to get one, though. From this realization, I got motivated to try and cad a plane. Considering that usually cading takes a long time, I thought this task was almost impossible. Slowly, however, I was able to create parts of the plane until after around 50 hours, the entire plane was complete.

The following are the STLs to all components of my final build. I did cut some of the components using the cutting tool in the prusaslicer when finally printing.

Under Vertical Stabilizer Horizontal Stab Supports Horizontal Stabilizer Mid Body Top Mid Body Bot Mid Body Support Wings Support Wings Connector Wings Tube Middle of Head Vertical Stabilizer Engine Cockpit Nose Flaps Back Landing Gears Front Gears



Here is the 3D model of the plane.



To print all the components took over 65 hours.

Surprisingly, there was really no code going on in the background. All the control surfaces are programmed into the remote control, which has built-in coding. All I did was calibrate everything and configure the settings.

After all the settings were configured, and the plane was printed, I put in all the electronics. These include the servos, motor, and transmitter.

The process of installing everything was tedious but easy. I placed the servos in place first, then fit the motor. I sautered the motor to the ESC, which leads to the battery. The ESC is also connected to the transmitter as well as all the servos. The servos are then connected via wire to the ailerons/flaps/rudder.


The front gear was a little bit different. I had to create two joint points so the gear would turn confidently.



Here is a look inside the tube of death.


Overall, the plane was great. It flew great when I could fly it. I just need to upgrade my landing gear.
